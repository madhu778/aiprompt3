A Quick Look at History

  • 1895: William G. Morgan invents "Mintonette" at a YMCA in Holyoke, MA, as a less strenuous alternative to basketball.
  • 1916: The "set" and "spike" are introduced in the Philippines, revolutionizing offensive strategy.
  • 1964: Volleyball makes its debut as an official Olympic sport at the Tokyo Games.

Master the Fundamentals & Beyond

Our training programs are designed to build a strong foundation and introduce advanced techniques to help you dominate the court.

Strength & Conditioning

Build explosive power and endurance. Focus on core stability, leg strength for higher jumps, and shoulder health for powerful spikes.

Agility & Footwork

Improve your court coverage and reaction time. We cover ladder drills, cone cuts, and defensive shuffling to make you faster and more efficient.

Court IQ & Positioning

Understand the game on a deeper level. Learn about rotational strategies, defensive positioning, and effective on-court communication.

Unconventional Tips for a Competitive Edge

  • Use colored cues for reaction drills to sharpen decision-making.
  • Practice "mirror" defense drills with music to vary tempo and improve adaptability.
  • Train on sand to significantly boost balance, stability, and jump height.
  • Play small-sided 2v2 games to maximize touches and improve ball control.
  • Incorporate throw-and-call drills to perfect on-court communication under pressure.
  • Play constraint-based games (e.g., one-touch only) to force quicker thinking.

Our Story

Volleyball Hobby was born from a simple idea: to create a single place for volleyball lovers to grow their skills and connect with others. We saw a need for a modern, accessible platform that caters to both the weekend warrior and the aspiring pro. Our mission is to fuel your passion for the sport.
